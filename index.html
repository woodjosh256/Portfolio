About

Four years ago, I started coding small games on my TI-84 graphing calculator. I quickly developed a passion for programming and loved how one could create nearly anything just by writing lines of code. I soon moved on to other more capable languages, primarily Python and Java. I began to spend a lot of my free time on small programming projects and even co-founded my high school’s coding club. I’ve decided that I want to turn my passion for programming into my career. I am now a freshman at UW-Platteville studying software engineering.

Outside of programming, I like to spend my time mountain biking, hiking/backpacking, and listening to music. A long-term goal of mine is to someday start my own business. Owning a company would ensure that the company I put all my effort into has a mission that I believe in. Another goal of mine is to thru-hike hike the Appalachian trail.
